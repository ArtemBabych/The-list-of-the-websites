Types of Axes

At Your Axe Club, we appreciate the diversity of axes and their unique purposes. Whether you're into precision throwing or crafting your own handles, we've got the right axes for you:

  • Throwing Axes: Perfect for honing your accuracy and strength in our axe-throwing competitions.
  • Craftsman's Axes: Ideal for those interested in crafting their own custom axe handles during our TimberCraft Workshops.
  • Double-Bit Axes: Versatile axes with two cutting edges, suitable for various chopping tasks.
  • Forest Ranger Axes: Designed for timber-cutting and outdoor adventures, these axes are essential for our wilderness retreats.

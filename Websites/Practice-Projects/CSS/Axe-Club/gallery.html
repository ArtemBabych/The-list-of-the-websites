Welcome to the Axe Enthusiasts Club!

Activities/Events

Explore our past and upcoming activities, events, and meet-ups. Join us for exciting adventures and connect with fellow axe enthusiasts!

Upcoming Events

  • Axe Fest 2024
    Date: August 15, 2024
    Location: Timberland Park
    Description: Axe Fest is our grand annual celebration of all things axe-related. Join us for a day filled with thrilling axe-throwing competitions, timber-cutting demonstrations, and a feast by the bonfire. It's an event you won't want to miss!
  • Night of a Thousand Swings
    Date: September 22, 2024
    Location: Moonlit Meadows
    Description: Experience the magic of axe swinging under the moonlight. Join us for a night of a thousand swings, illuminated by the stars. Enjoy axe-throwing challenges, live music, and a midnight barbecue.

Past Events

    Date: June 5, 2023
    Location: Craftsmanship Hall
    Description: TimberCraft Workshop was an educational day where members learned the art of crafting custom axe handles. Participants left with personalized axes, showcasing their craftsmanship skills.
  • Axe and Chill Retreat
    Date: May 12-15, 2023
    Location: Wilderness Haven Resort
    Description: Axe and Chill Retreat was a weekend getaway filled with relaxation and axe-related activities. Highlights included axe-throwing by the lake, forest hikes, and evening bonfires.
